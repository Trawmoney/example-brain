Whenever you hear…
I “have to” XYZ…
You “must” XYZ…
Society “should” XYZ…
They “need to” XYZ…

…remember that an implied threat to self or the universe follows.

If you finish the implied threat, you can figure out if you REALLY “have to,” or just prefer it.

Words matter.
